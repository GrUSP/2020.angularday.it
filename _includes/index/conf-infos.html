Who is it for?

Angular Day is dedicated to developers and technical managers.

Each year, you can learn about all the updates on Angular, find new partners and meet new peers and employees.

What is it about?

We will talk about new development methodologies, best practices, success stories, code quality, data architectures, modularization, performance and much more. The conference is organized by GrUSP, the organizers of jsDay.
